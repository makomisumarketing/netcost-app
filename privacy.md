# Privacy Policy for NetCost

Last updated: March 15, 2026

NetCost is designed to help users track their recurring cost of living. Your privacy matters to us.

## Information We Collect

NetCost does not collect, store, or access the financial information you enter into the app.

All data you enter into NetCost is stored locally on your device, and may also be stored in your personal iCloud account if iCloud syncing is enabled on your device.

The developer does not have access to this data.

## Accounts

NetCost does not require you to create an account to use the app.

If iCloud is used for syncing or backup, that service is provided by Apple through your own Apple account.

## Analytics and Tracking

NetCost does not use its own analytics or tracking systems to monitor your personal financial data.

If the app includes advertisements, third-party advertising providers may collect limited device or usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

## Advertising

NetCost may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising partners such as Google AdMob. These providers may collect limited device information, advertising identifiers, and usage data in order to serve and measure advertisements. This data is processed according to the privacy policies of those providers.

## Third-Party Services

NetCost may use third-party services such as Apple iCloud and advertising providers.

These services may process limited technical data as needed to provide their functionality.

## Data Storage

Your entered data is stored:

- locally on your device  
- optionally in your personal iCloud storage, if enabled

The developer does not maintain external servers for storing your financial data.

## Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by using the reset or delete option inside the app.

If your data is stored in iCloud, removal may also depend on your Apple account and iCloud settings.

## Children's Privacy

NetCost is not directed to children under 13, and the app is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
